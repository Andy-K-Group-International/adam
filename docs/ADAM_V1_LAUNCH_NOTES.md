# A.D.A.M. v1.0 — Controlled SaaS License Launch Notes

## Positioning

A.D.A.M. v1.0 is a controlled SaaS license launch. External companies can apply for access. Andy'K Group manually reviews, approves and activates each client. This is intentional — not a limitation.

---

## What v1.0 Supports

- External companies apply via `/questionnaire`
- Andy'K Group reviews each application manually
- Founding Client Program — first 20 companies, max 20% benefit
- Payment via Revolut (activated 15 July 2026)
- Manual business verification per client
- Manual activation after verification
- Admin-controlled client lifecycle
- Full client lifecycle system (proposal → contract → invoice → activation)
- 15 email templates
- AI assistance (basic mode, Anthropic)
- Health scores, referral tracking, cron reminders

---

## What v1.0 Does NOT Support Yet

- Company Admin role (each licensed company managing their own clients)
- Multi-tenant isolation (Company A cannot see Company B — but no Company B exists yet)
- Self-service Company Admin onboarding
- White-label branding per company
- Company-specific AI settings controlled by Company Admin
- Automated payment-to-activation flow (manual approval required)

---

## Intentional v1.0 Decisions

- Single admin (`ceo@andykgroup.com`) controls all clients
- All Founding Clients are managed directly by Andy'K Group
- Activation is manual — ensures quality control at launch
- Payment guard active until 15 July 2026
- AI API key storage disabled — pending encrypted implementation

---

## Official Launch Date

**15 July 2026**
